Fun facts :

  • In the absence of gravity, the vertebrae in an astronaut's spine can expand and relax, leading to a temporary increase in height.
  • While not baby diapers, astronauts wear "Maximum Absorbency Garments" (MAGs) within their spacesuits for urination
  • Astronauts experience a rapid sequence of sunrises and sunsets, approximately 15 times a day in space, due to the Earth's rotation.
  • In the zero-gravity environment of space, tears won't fall down the face, but will instead remain in the form of a sphere.
  • Some astronauts, especially those involved with the International Space Station (ISS), must learn Russian to understand and work on the Russian modules of the ISS.
